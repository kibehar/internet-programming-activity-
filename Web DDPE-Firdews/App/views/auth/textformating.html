Addis Ababa Science and Technology University

Addis Ababa Science and Technology University(Amharic:Addis Ababa Science and Technology University), or A.A.S.T.U is an Ethhiopian higher institute in Addis ababa, Ethiopia. The main campus located around kilinto area.

Currently Astu AASTU has enrolled more than 8000 undergraduate(under regular continuing education program) and close to 700 postgraduate studentits nine applied science, technology engineering and ICT focused school AASTU is a university in the making and much of its short-term plans aim at established academic infrastructure and facilities, staff recurrenting and manppwer development So far, We managed to recruit 472 academic staff and 39 administrative staff.[1][2]
